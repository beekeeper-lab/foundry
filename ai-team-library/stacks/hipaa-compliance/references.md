## Key Legislation

| Law | Year | Significance |
|-----|------|-------------|
| **Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA)** | 1996 | Established national standards for protecting health information |
| **Health Information Technology for Economic and Clinical Health (HITECH) Act** | 2009 | Strengthened HIPAA enforcement, added breach notification requirements, extended liability to business associates |
| **Omnibus Rule** | 2013 | Implemented HITECH provisions, modified breach standard, expanded business associate obligations |

## Key Concepts Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Covered Entity (CE)** | Health plan, healthcare clearinghouse, or healthcare provider that transmits health information electronically |
| **Business Associate (BA)** | Person or entity that creates, receives, maintains, or transmits PHI on behalf of a covered entity |
| **Protected Health Information (PHI)** | Individually identifiable health information in any form (electronic, paper, oral) |
| **Electronic PHI (ePHI)** | PHI that is created, received, maintained, or transmitted in electronic form |
| **Unsecured PHI** | PHI not rendered unusable, unreadable, or indecipherable through encryption or destruction |
| **Minimum Necessary** | Standard requiring that only the minimum amount of PHI needed for the purpose be used, disclosed, or requested |
| **De-Identification** | Removal of identifiers such that information no longer identifies an individual (Safe Harbor or Expert Determination) |
| **Required (R)** | Security Rule specification that must be implemented |
| **Addressable (A)** | Security Rule specification that must be assessed — implement, implement alternative, or document why not reasonable |
