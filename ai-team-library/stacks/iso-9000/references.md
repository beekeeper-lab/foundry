# References and Resources

Authoritative sources for ISO 9000 family standards, quality management guidance,
and third-party resources that agents can use to build and verify quality
management knowledge.

---

## ISO Standards (Primary)

| Standard | Title | Scope |
|----------|-------|-------|
| **ISO 9000:2015** | Quality management systems — Fundamentals and vocabulary | Defines the core concepts, principles, and terminology used across the ISO 9000 family |
| **ISO 9001:2015** | Quality management systems — Requirements | The certifiable standard. Specifies requirements for a QMS when an organization needs to demonstrate its ability to consistently provide conforming products/services |
| **ISO 9004:2018** | Quality management — Quality of an organization — Guidance to achieve sustained success | Goes beyond ISO 9001 to address the quality of the organization as a whole, including its ability to achieve sustained success |
| **ISO 19011:2018** | Guidelines for auditing management systems | Provides guidance on planning and conducting audits of management systems, including QMS audits |

### How to Access ISO Standards

- **Purchase:** ISO standards are available from [iso.org](https://www.iso.org)
  and national standards bodies (ANSI in the US, BSI in the UK)
- **ANSI Webstore:** [webstore.ansi.org](https://webstore.ansi.org) — US
  distribution for ISO standards
- **Preview:** ISO provides free publicly available sections (scope, normative
  references, terms) but the full text requires purchase
- **Library access:** Many university and corporate libraries provide access to
  ISO standards

## Related Standards and Frameworks

| Standard/Framework | Relationship to ISO 9001 | When to Consider |
|-------------------|--------------------------|------------------|
| **ISO 14001** (Environmental Management) | Shares Annex SL structure with 9001 — can be integrated | When environmental management is required alongside quality |
| **ISO 45001** (Occupational Health & Safety) | Shares Annex SL structure — integrates with 9001 | When workplace safety management is needed |
| **ISO 27001** (Information Security) | Shares Annex SL structure — integrates with 9001 | When information security management is required |
| **ISO 31000** (Risk Management) | Risk management framework — supports 9001's risk-based thinking | When a formal risk management framework is needed beyond 9001's requirements |
| **CMMI** (Capability Maturity Model Integration) | Process maturity model — complementary to ISO 9001 | When measuring process maturity levels across the organization |
| **Six Sigma** | Statistical quality improvement methodology | When data-driven process improvement is a priority |
| **Lean** | Waste elimination and value stream optimization | When efficiency and waste reduction are primary goals |
| **COSO Framework** | Internal control framework — complements QMS controls | When integrating quality management with internal controls (relevant for SOX) |

## Key Quality Management Terminology (ISO 9000:2015)

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Quality** | Degree to which a set of inherent characteristics of an object fulfils requirements |
| **Quality Management System (QMS)** | Management system to direct and control an organization with regard to quality |
| **Nonconformity** | Non-fulfillment of a requirement |
| **Corrective Action** | Action to eliminate the cause of a nonconformity and to prevent recurrence |
| **Continual Improvement** | Recurring activity to enhance performance |
| **Competence** | Ability to apply knowledge and skills to achieve intended results |
| **Documented Information** | Information required to be controlled and maintained by an organization |
| **Process** | Set of interrelated or interacting activities which transforms inputs into outputs |
| **Audit** | Systematic, independent, and documented process for obtaining audit evidence and evaluating it objectively |
| **Management Review** | Evaluation of the suitability, adequacy, effectiveness, and efficiency of the management system by top management |
